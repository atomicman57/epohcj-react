ABOUT US

PhantFashion Co. Ltd. is the ideal online fashion directory aimed at building a bridge between fashion designers, dealers and their prospective foreign and local market. This platform encourages and expands creativity in the Fashion industry by providing Fashion dealers and designers with a business junction which exposes their professionalism, craftmanship and products to prospective clients. Phantfashion also provides a wide range of fashion seller services that will help entrepreneurs in the clothing and textile sector to manage and grow their business online. Phantfashion as part of its objectives, help showcase the exceptional work of fashion stylists on our website for potential customers to see, thereby making business transaction possible and beneficial to both parties desires.

The birthing of PhantFashion was made a reality in 2017 after years were invested by both founders in preparation, knowledge and resource gathering towards the launch of their vision. It finally became the brand PHANTFASHION, which is fast living up to its potential.

The phantfashion brand is one that portrays the expectations placed on the brand name itself and the possibility of it becoming an household name in the fashion industry. Two words which will give its customers and users the perfect image of one of the largest fashion Hub to emerge, they are; 'PHANT' from the animal 'ELEPHANT' and 'Fashion' thereby giving it PHANTFASHION' translating to mean THE BIG FASHION HUB.
