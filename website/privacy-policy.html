PRIVACY POLICY

YOUR DETAILS

To place an order with us online, registration is required. At the point of registration, we request certain information including your name, delivery and email address and payment card details. The information is required to enable us process your order and notify you of its progress.

INFORMATION WE USE, HOW & WHY

Once an order has been placed we will contact you by email to confirm your order details and again once your order has been accepted and dispatched. Should we need to contact you for any reason regarding your order, we will use email address registered to your account, or telephone number where provided.

After you have registered with us, we would also like to send you details of our latest products and special offers.

With exception of feedback requests or assistance with the delivery, we will never pass your email address to any other organization (or third party). For the sole purpose of aiding a delivery, your contact details may be shared with the chosen carrier.
